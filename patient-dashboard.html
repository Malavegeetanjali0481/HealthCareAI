Help Desk & Support
Answers to common questions and quick assistance
How do I book a teleconsultation?
Go to "Check Your Symptoms", complete the symptom analysis, and click "Talk to a Doctor" when your result shows Medium or High risk. You will be connected to an available doctor.
How can I contact my ASHA Worker?
Click on "ASHA Worker Details" in the left menu. You will see your assigned ASHA worker's name, phone number, and a direct call button.
What does the AI Risk Level mean?
🟢 Low Risk: Manage at home with rest and fluids. 🟡 Medium Risk: Speak to a doctor within 24 hours. 🔴 High Risk: Visit a health facility immediately or call 108.
Is my health data safe and private?
Yes. All data is protected under the DPDP Act 2023 and ABDM guidelines. Your information is encrypted and only accessible to authorized healthcare professionals.

Health Education
Tips, prevention guides, and seasonal alerts
Seasonal Alert — Dengue Season:

Dengue cases are rising in Barwani district. Keep water containers covered. Use mosquito repellent. Check for fever above 102°F and rash. Visit PHC immediately if symptoms appear.

💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses of clean, boiled water every day. Dehydration worsens most illnesses, especially in summer months.
🍎
Balanced Diet
Include fruits, vegetables, and lentils in every meal. Good nutrition strengthens your immunity against seasonal diseases.
🧼
Hand Hygiene
Wash hands with soap for 20 seconds before eating, after using the toilet, and after touching animals. This prevents 80% of infections.
💉
Stay Vaccinated
Ensure all children under 5 are vaccinated as per the national schedule. Check with your ASHA worker for due dates. Vaccines save lives.
🌙
Sleep Well
Adults need 7–8 hours of sleep per night. Good sleep helps your body repair, boosts immunity, and reduces stress-related illness.
🚶
Daily Exercise
Even a 30-minute walk reduces risk of diabetes, heart disease, and depression. Encourage the whole family to stay active.
