Ethical, Responsible Technology

We build technology that elevates healthcare professionals, ensuring smart tools work for India without compromising safety or sovereignty.

  • Assists, Does Not Replace: Technology empowers frontline health workers — final clinical judgment always remains with a human doctor.
  • No Autonomous Diagnoses: All system outputs are decision-support recommendations only, never final diagnoses.
  • Privacy-First Design: Patient data stored in India, encrypted end-to-end, with explicit consent flows.
  • Explainable Decisions: All outputs include clear reasoning trails for doctors and officials to review and override.
Assistive Model (Not Autonomous): This system follows the NHP-mandated "Human-in-the-Loop" principle for all clinical decisions.
