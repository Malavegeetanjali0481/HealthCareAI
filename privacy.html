Data Privacy & Ethical AI

Our commitment to responsible, transparent, and ethical healthcare AI

AI Does Not Replace Doctors

  • The AI system provides decision support only.
  • It does not diagnose diseases or prescribe medications.
  • Final medical decisions are made by qualified healthcare professionals.

User Consent & Data Collection

Clear explanation of what data is collected:

  • Basic patient details
  • Symptoms entered
  • AI risk assessment results
Note: No data is collected without explicit user consent.

Data Anonymization & Security

  • Personally identifiable information is anonymized.
  • Data is encrypted in storage and transit.
  • Role-based access control limits data visibility.
  • Only authorized healthcare personnel can view sensitive data.

How Data Is Used

Improve triage accuracy
Support public health monitoring
Optimize telemedicine workflows
Data is never sold or used for non-healthcare purposes.

Ethical AI Principles

  • Human-in-the-loop decision making
  • Explainable AI outputs
  • Bias-aware model design
  • Safe escalation for high-risk cases
  • Transparency in AI usage

User Rights

  • Right to access personal data
  • Right to request data deletion
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to transparency

Compliance & Best Practices

Aligned with healthcare data protection best practices

Designed with privacy-by-design principles

Suitable for adaptation to local health regulations

"Ethical AI builds trust.
Trust builds better healthcare."
